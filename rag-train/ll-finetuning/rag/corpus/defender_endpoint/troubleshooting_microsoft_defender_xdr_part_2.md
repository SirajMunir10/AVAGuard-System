**Domain:** Defender for Endpoint

## Scenario / Query
How to use the Investigation graph tab to understand the full scope of an attack in a Microsoft Defender XDR incident?

## Environment Context
- **Tenant Type:** Microsoft 365 Defender
- **Configuration:** N/A

## Symptoms
- Need to understand the connection of alerts to impacted assets
- Need to see which entities are related to which alerts and how they are part of the attack story

N/A

## Root Causes
N/A

## Remediation Steps
1. Navigate to the Investigation graph tab of the incident
2. Review the connection of alerts to the impacted assets in your organization
3. Identify which entities are related to which alerts and how they are part of the story of the attack
4. Review the alerts for the incident

## Validation
The investigation graph helps you quickly understand the full scope of the attack by connecting the different suspicious entities that are part of the attack with their related assets such as users, devices, and mailboxes
