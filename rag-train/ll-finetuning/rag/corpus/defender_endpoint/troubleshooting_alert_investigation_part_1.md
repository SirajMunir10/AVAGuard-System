# Troubleshooting: Alert Investigation

**Domain:** Defender for Endpoint
**Subdomain:** Alert Investigation
**Incident Type:** Troubleshooting

## Scenario / Query
How to investigate a Defender alert using the alert story to understand why it was triggered and review related events?

## Environment Context
- **Tenant Type:** Microsoft 365 Defender
- **Configuration:** Alert story feature enabled

## Symptoms
- Alert triggered in Microsoft Defender for Endpoint
- Need to understand the sequence of events leading to the alert

## Error Codes
N/A

## Root Causes
N/A

## Remediation Steps
1. Open the alert story to view why the alert was triggered
2. Review related events that occurred before and after the alert
3. Click on entities to expand and view details at a glance
4. Select an entity to switch the context of the details pane to that entity
5. Use the '...' menu on the entity card to see all available actions for that entity

## Validation
The entity in focus is indicated by a blue stripe on the left side of its card, with the alert in the title being in focus initially
