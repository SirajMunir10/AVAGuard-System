60.1: (L2) Ensure 'Enable news and interests' is set to 'Not

Overview
This policy setting specifies whether the news and interests feature is allowed on the 
device. 
The recommended state for this setting is: Not Allowed. 
Note: This setting only applies to Windows 10. When configured in settings catalog for 
a Windows 11 system an error will be generated in both Intune and the local event log.

Why This Matters
Due to privacy concerns, apps and features such as news and interests on the 
Windows taskbar should be treated as a possible security risk due to the potential of 
data being sent back to third-parties, such as Microsoft. 
In addition, the app may display inappropriate news and interests within the feed.

Audit Steps
Navigate to the UI Path articulated in the Remediation section and confirm it is set as 
prescribed. This policy setting is backed by the following registry location with a 
REG_DWORD value of 0. 
HKLM\SOFTWARE\Policies\Microsoft\Windows\Windows Feeds:EnableFeeds

Remediation
To establish the recommended configuration via configuration profiles, set the following 
Settings Catalog path to Not Allowed: 
News and interests\Enable News and interests 
Note: At time of benchmark publication, the Feeds category was not visible when 
browsing by category. To find this setting, search for news and feeds, and the Feeds 
category will show.

Key Takeaways
- Due to privacy concerns, apps and features such as news and interests on the  Windows taskbar should be treated as a possible security risk due to the potential of  data being sent back to third-parties, such as Microsoft.
- In addition, the app may display inappropriate news and interests within the feed.
