# Implementation: Insider Risk Management

**Domain:** Purview
**Subdomain:** Insider Risk Management
**Incident Type:** Implementation

## Scenario / Query
How do I customize which columns are displayed or hidden in the Insider Risk Management cases view?

## Environment Context
- **Tenant Type:** Purview
- **Configuration:** Insider Risk Management cases page

## Symptoms
N/A

## Error Codes
N/A

## Root Causes
N/A

## Remediation Steps
1. On the right side of the page, select Customize columns.
2. Select or clear the checkboxes for the columns you want to display or hide.

## Validation
1. Navigate to Microsoft Purview compliance portal > Insider Risk Management > Cases. 2. On the right side of the page, select 'Customize columns'. 3. Verify that the checkboxes for the columns you intend to display are selected, and those you intend to hide are cleared. 4. Select 'Apply' and confirm the cases view now shows only the selected columns.

## Rollback
1. Navigate to Microsoft Purview compliance portal > Insider Risk Management > Cases. 2. On the right side of the page, select 'Customize columns'. 3. Restore the original column selection by selecting the checkboxes for the previously displayed columns and clearing those for the previously hidden columns. 4. Select 'Apply' to revert to the original columns view.
